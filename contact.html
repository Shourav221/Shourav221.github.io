Q: What is eSports Management?

A: eSports Management involves organizing, coordinating, and overseeing the activities and operations of eSports teams, tournaments, and events.

Q: How do I create an account?

A: Click on the "Register Now" button and fill out the registration form with your details. You'll receive a confirmation email to activate your account.

Q: I forgot my password. What should I do?

A: Click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page and follow the instructions to reset your password.

Q: What features are available in the eSports Management Dashboard?

A: Our dashboard offers tools for team performance tracking, tournament scheduling, player communication, and more.

Q: How can I manage my team's roster?

A: After logging in, go to the "Team Management" section where you can add, remove, or edit player profiles.
